An add-on for Google Slides TM


1. Swap objects

Switch the position of two items in one click. Not available by default!

2. Dot-dash to slides

Select a text box with your outline. Click once to get all the titles and sticky notes. This is how the pros "ghost" a deck.

3. Insert text-box tables

With just one click, insert a grid of adjustable text-boxes (aka Marvin Table).

4. And much more!

Other features incldue inserting Harvey Balls, resetting object rotation, resizing multiple objects, alignment, etc... and we're going to keep adding more shortcuts that are otherwise not available.
